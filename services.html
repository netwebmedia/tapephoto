Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you cover?

I'm based in Coquimbo and work across La Serena, Coquimbo and the whole Coquimbo Region in Chile. Sessions elsewhere in Chile are possible — travel is quoted separately.

How long until I receive my photos?

Turnaround depends on the session type and the season — you'll get a concrete delivery date in your quote when you book. If you need a small selection sooner for social media, ask when booking.

Can I use the photos commercially?

Usage licensing is agreed per project and stated in your quote — typically covering your own business marketing: website, social media, listings and printed material. Extended licensing (resale, advertising campaigns for third parties) is quoted separately.

Do you travel outside the Coquimbo Region?

Yes. For locations outside the region, travel time and expenses are added to the quote. Tell me the location and date and I'll send a full price up front — no surprises.

Are drone flights allowed at my location?

Drone flights are planned according to DGAC (Chilean civil aviation) rules and the conditions of each site. Some locations — near airports, over crowds or in protected areas — may need extra permits and lead time, so mention your location when you get in touch.
